My research pioneers beyond conventional paradigms, delving into the fundamental nature of intelligence and understanding.

Discover My Story

Research Through AI-Human Collaboration: A Systematic Approach

At 19, I've developed a growing portfolio of academic publications through a systematic methodology that leverages AI as a research partner rather than merely a tool. My approach represents a principled integration of human insight with AI-powered synthesis and implementation.

Technical Foundation

Through structured, self-directed study using AI as a Socratic dialogue partner, I've developed deep expertise in the mathematical foundations underlying modern AI systems—advanced linear algebra, optimization theory, Bayesian inference, information theory, and the theoretical underpinnings of transformer architectures. My learning is driven by an obsession with first principles: I don't just want to know how something works, but why it works. This philosophical commitment to understanding fundamental mechanisms has enabled me to engage with graduate-level AI research while building systems from theoretical foundations to production implementations.

Core Methodology

My research workflow integrates human conceptual leadership with AI collaboration: I formulate hypotheses and design architectural frameworks, leverage AI for comprehensive literature synthesis and initial implementations, then rigorously validate, optimize, and debug all technical components. This approach enables research velocity while maintaining complete technical accountability.

Research Philosophy

My work is unified by a dual focus on interpretability and uncertainty quantification—not as separate domains, but as complementary approaches to the fundamental challenge of understanding intelligence. These principles shape how I approach every technical problem, from model architectures to training dynamics.

Current Focus

I investigate the intersection of interpretability and uncertainty quantification, exploring how we can build AI systems that are both understandable and appropriately calibrated in their confidence.

Key Research Areas

Mechanistic Interpretability & Targeted Model Control

I pioneer methods to deeply understand large AI models, identifying neural circuits responsible for specific knowledge or behaviors. Leveraging these insights, I design surgical interventions to precisely modify or enhance model capabilities for unparalleled control.

Dynamic Knowledge Management & Cross-Lingual Understanding in LLMs

My work addresses static knowledge challenges in LLMs through adaptive strategies for dynamic updates and catastrophic forgetting mitigation. I also explore propagating latent cross-lingual knowledge in multilingual LLMs to enhance low-resource language alignment.

Real-time Control & Semantic Stability in Generative Models

I develop innovative frameworks for real-time monitoring and adaptive guidance of complex generative processes like diffusion models. This research aims to enhance output quality, improve prompt adherence, reduce artifacts, and provide fine-grained control over generation.

Uncertainty Quantification for Robust & Controllable AI

My research investigates methodologies to quantify and leverage model uncertainty, enhancing control mechanisms in complex AI systems. The goal is to integrate explicit uncertainty signals into adaptive frameworks, building more reliable and auditable AI that knows what it doesn't know.
