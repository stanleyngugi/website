About Me: Decoding Reality, Building the Future

I am a deep, integrative thinker at the intersection of technology, philosophy, and human experience. My work is driven by an insatiable curiosity about the underlying principles that govern our reality, and how we can better understand and build within it. I believe that true insight emerges not from confined specializations, but from connecting seemingly disparate fields – from physics to spirituality, neuroscience to mathematics, biology to AI research – seeing them as different languages describing the same fundamental truth.

My passion for Artificial Intelligence goes beyond surface-level application. I'm fascinated by the very nature of intelligence itself, exploring how machines can develop genuine understanding, how uncertainty fuels creativity, and how we can design AI that truly augments human cognition. I'm particularly drawn to pushing the boundaries, questioning concepts like fixed weights and exploring the potential for non-linguistic thought in AI, believing that machines don't need to be conscious to engage in profound inquiry. This deep understanding of uncertainty is crucial, especially when designing AI for high-risk applications where knowing when a system is unsure is paramount.

I am a self-directed explorer and a proactive problem-solver. My learning journey is characterized by diving deep into concepts. I thrive on identifying and dismantling hidden complexities, whether it's understanding the nuances of an AI framework or navigating the new bottleneck of human comprehension in an age of infinite digital content. I firmly believe that in a world of abundant information, the most valuable skill is the courage to question everything.

Ultimately, I am building towards a future where technology is deeply understood, ethically developed, and harmoniously integrated with our most fundamental human capacities, including the rich power of our common senses. I offer a unique blend of philosophical depth, technical acumen, and an unwavering commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible, always seeking to learn, create, and explore "what is it like" beyond the known.

Key Research Areas

Mechanistic Interpretability & Targeted Model Control

I pioneer methods to deeply understand large AI models, identifying neural circuits responsible for specific knowledge or behaviors. Leveraging these insights, I design surgical interventions to precisely modify or enhance model capabilities for unparalleled control.

Dynamic Knowledge Management & Cross-Lingual Understanding in LLMs

My work addresses static knowledge challenges in LLMs through adaptive strategies for dynamic updates and catastrophic forgetting mitigation. I also explore propagating latent cross-lingual knowledge in multilingual LLMs to enhance low-resource language alignment.

Real-time Control & Semantic Stability in Generative Models

I develop innovative frameworks for real-time monitoring and adaptive guidance of complex generative processes like diffusion models. This research aims to enhance output quality, improve prompt adherence, reduce artifacts, and provide fine-grained control over generation.

Uncertainty Quantification for Robust & Controllable AI

My research investigates methodologies to quantify and leverage model uncertainty, enhancing control mechanisms in complex AI systems. The goal is to integrate explicit uncertainty signals into adaptive frameworks, building more reliable and auditable AI that knows what it doesn't know.
